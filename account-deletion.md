# Account Deletion Request — TimeHunt

If you have signed in to TimeHunt using your Google or Apple account and would like to delete your account and associated data, follow the steps below.

---

## What data is stored?

| Data type | Where stored | Deleted when |
|---|---|---|
| Game progress, settings, level data | Locally on your device | When you uninstall the app |
| Google/Apple sign-in profile (name, email) | Google / Apple servers | Upon account deletion request |
| Ad interaction data | Google AdMob | Per Google's data retention policy |

---

## How to request account deletion

### Option 1 — Email request (all users)

Send an email to **[your-email@example.com]** with the subject line:

> **TimeHunt Account Deletion Request**

Include the following in your email:
- The email address associated with your Google or Apple account
- A brief note confirming you want your account and data deleted

We will process your request within **30 days** and confirm via email.

### Option 2 — Remove local data yourself

1. Open your device **Settings**
2. Go to **Apps → TimeHunt**
3. Tap **Clear Data**

This removes all locally stored game progress immediately.

---

## What happens after deletion

- Your Google/Apple sign-in association with TimeHunt is removed
- Any profile data we hold is permanently deleted within **30 days**
- Ad interaction data held by Google AdMob is subject to [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Local device data is removed when you clear app data or uninstall
